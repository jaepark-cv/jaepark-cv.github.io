I am a Ph.D. candidate in Hospitality and Tourism Management at Purdue University, with a strong commitment to understanding consumer behavior in an increasingly digital landscape. My primary research centers on **digital customer experience**. My dissertation, *Customer Journey in the AI Era: Touchpoints with Digital Platforms Across the
Pre-, During-, and Post-Travel Stages*, examines how AI-powered touchpoints shape customer decision-making and behavioral intentions across the customer journey.

In addition to my foundational research, I am passionate about applying consumer behavior insights to contribute to real-world challenges across the service industry. I am expanding my research to **social sustainability**, such as supporting local businesses through technology-enhanced services. These projects reflect my broader goal of connecting behavioral and psychological theory with practical impact across diverse touchpoints in the modern customer experience ecosystem.
